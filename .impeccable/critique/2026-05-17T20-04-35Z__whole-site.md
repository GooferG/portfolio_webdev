## Anti-Patterns Verdict

Site lands flush in the AI-slop dev-portfolio lane (first-order). Dark navy + teal + WebGL shader + pulsing pill + uppercase tracking-[3px] eyebrow + Inter + framer-motion FadeIn-on-everything. Pattern-matches in 3 seconds. Second-order: eyebrow pattern repeats on 7+ page headers — stops meaning "section," starts meaning "template." The dotted-i and chrome-flow shader are real differentiation; everything around them is template-shaped.

PRODUCT.md itself names this as the #1 risk. Site occupies the lane competently but does not earn its place in it.

**Deterministic scan:** 2 findings (`bg-black` × 2 on `components/ui/ProjectDetailClient.tsx:83, :193`). Ran in `--fast` mode.

## What's Working
- DottedI component — defensible signature
- Chrome-flow WebGL shader — custom, performant
- Experience timeline with leading-zero counters — strongest "designed for reading" surface

## Priority Issues

### [P0] Hero + homepage land in AI-slop reflex-reject lane
**Why:** PRODUCT.md flags as #1 risk. Portfolio IS the work sample; pattern-match in 3s = lost conversion.
**Fix:** Drop shader from first paint OR replace dual-pill+typewriter with one opinionated line OR move teal off eyebrows.
**Cmd:** `/impeccable bolder hero`

### [P0] Eyebrow pattern repeats 7+ times
**Why:** Strongest single tell of templated production.
**Fix:** Keep on at most 2 surfaces. Strip elsewhere. Lean into Experience H1 split-italic voice as template.
**Cmd:** `/impeccable typeset page-titles`

### [P1] Services has no scope/pricing/per-card CTA
**Why:** Help scored 1/4. Buyer can't gauge fit in 30s. Bounces.
**Fix:** Each card gets scope+timeline+CTA. Add "How I work" 3-step.
**Cmd:** `/impeccable clarify services`

### [P1] Contact success state flat; failure has no mailto link
**Why:** Form completion is the only guaranteed peak; one-line ack wastes it.
**Fix:** Fuller success block (24h promise + Cal.com + CV nudge). Real mailto on error. Field-level email validation.
**Cmd:** `/impeccable harden contact-form`

### [P2] Motion pile-up violates "one moment of texture"
**Why:** Site ships 7+ moments. Reads as junior, contradicts senior brand.
**Fix:** Keep shader + dotted-i. Cut typewriter, shimmer-pill, 3 of 4 pulses, group-hover scale, FadeIn-on-visible.
**Cmd:** `/impeccable quieter site-wide`
